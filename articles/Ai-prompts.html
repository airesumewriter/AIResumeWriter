AI Resume Prompts That Actually Work

Published: July 6, 2025 | Author: AI Resume Writer Team

AI can save you hours when writing a resume‚Äîbut only if you give it the right prompts. Here are proven prompt formulas that generate interview-winning content.

1. Ask for Role-Specific Summaries

Prompt: "Write a professional summary for a [Job Title] with 5+ years of experience in [Industry], skilled in [Skill 1, Skill 2]."

2. Generate Bullet Points from Experience

Prompt: "Turn this job experience into 3 resume bullets that highlight achievements: [Paste your job tasks]."

3. Reword for Impact

Prompt: "Make this resume sentence more powerful and achievement-driven: [Paste sentence]."

4. Add Keywords from Job Ads

Prompt: "Add these keywords into my resume summary naturally: [List of keywords]."

5. Tailor to a Job Description

Prompt: "Rewrite my resume for this job description: [Paste resume] [Paste job ad]."

6. Build a Skills Section

Prompt: "Based on this job ad, list the top 10 skills to include in my resume. [Paste job ad]"

7. Convert Career Gaps Into Strengths

Prompt: "Write a sentence explaining a 1-year career gap due to caregiving in a positive and professional way."
