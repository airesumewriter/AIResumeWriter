10 Proven Ways to Beat the Applicant Tracking System (ATS)

Published: July 6, 2025 | Author: AI Resume Writer Team

1. Use Standard Fonts and Formatting

Stick to readable fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman. Avoid tables, text boxes, or columns that may confuse ATS scanners.

Use exact keywords found in the job ad. Many ATS systems score resumes based on keyword matching.
