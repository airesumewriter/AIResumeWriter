AI Resume Writing Prompts That Instantly Upgrade Your CV

Published July 2025 • By AIResumeWriter Team

AI tools are only as good as the prompts you give them. If you want your resume to truly shine, try feeding your AI writer these field-tested instructions:

🎯 For Bullet Points

  • “Write achievement-based bullet points for a sales associate who boosted monthly revenue by 25%.”
  • “Generate 3 impactful action statements for a teacher who integrated AI tools into the classroom.”

📝 For Resume Summaries

  • “Summarize a digital marketer’s experience into a 2-sentence resume headline with measurable outcomes.”
  • “Create a compelling resume summary for a job changer moving from teaching to HR.”

💡 Bonus Tip

Always include context, achievements, and job titles in your prompt. The clearer your prompt, the better the results!
